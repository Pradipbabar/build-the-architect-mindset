## Important Notes

### Time Tracking Rules
1. **Be honest**: Log ACTUAL time, not aspirational
2. **Be specific**: Say what you did, not just "learning"
3. **Be daily**: Log every day, don't batch on Sunday
4. **No gap rule**: Never go 2+ weeks without logging

### What Counts as "Logged Time"
✅ **Counts**:
- Active coding/building
- Reading technical books (with notes)
- Working through exercises
- Doing projects
- Reviewing code
- Researching specific technical topic
- Writing documentation

❌ **Doesn't Count**:
- Scrolling Twitter/YouTube
- Watching videos without taking notes
- "Thinking about coding"
- Reading news articles
- Passive watching

### Where to Store This
- Create weekly files in `/roadmap/time-tracking/`
- Name: `week-2026-03-21.md`
- Enable easy searching and historical review
