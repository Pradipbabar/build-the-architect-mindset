## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "I don't have time for 10 hours/week"
→ This roadmap isn't for you right now. Revisit when you can commit.

### "I don't know Go yet"
→ That's the whole point of Phase 1! Start with Tour of Go.

### "I'm overwhelmed"
→ Normal. Just do this week's tasks. Ignore the rest. Come back after Week 1.

### "Should I buy the Go book today?"
→ No. Do Tour of Go first (it's free). Buy book in Week 2 if you like it.

### "Can I use AI to help?"
→ For understanding concepts: YES. For building projects: NO. Read [COMMITMENT.md](COMMITMENT.md).
