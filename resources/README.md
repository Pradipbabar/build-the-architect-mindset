# Learning Resources

This directory contains curated resources to support your journey to becoming a Principal Engineer. Resources are organized by type and include books, courses, articles, and tools.

## Resource Categories

### Books (books.md)
- Essential reading for technical and leadership development
- Curated selection of industry-standard and influential works
- Organized by topic areas (architecture, leadership, technology)

### Courses (courses.md)
- Online courses and certifications
- University-level and professional training programs
- Self-paced and instructor-led learning options

### Articles (articles.md)
- Key articles, papers, and blog posts
- Industry insights and research findings
- Practical guides and case studies

### Tools (tools.md)
- Development tools and platforms
- Productivity and collaboration software
- Learning and career development resources

## How to Use These Resources

1. **Assessment**: Review your current skill level and identify gaps
2. **Planning**: Select resources that align with your current phase
3. **Scheduling**: Allocate dedicated time for learning activities
4. **Application**: Apply new knowledge through projects and practice
5. **Review**: Regularly assess progress and adjust your learning plan

## Learning Strategy

- **Active Learning**: Don't just read - apply concepts through projects
- **Spaced Repetition**: Review key concepts regularly over time
- **Teaching Others**: Explain concepts to reinforce understanding
- **Community**: Join study groups and discussion forums

## Contribution Guidelines

- Suggest new resources through pull requests
- Include rationale for recommendations
- Focus on high-quality, authoritative sources
- Keep resources current and relevant
